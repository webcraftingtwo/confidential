01
Executive Summary

The Valterra SLAM Re-Entry App is a purpose-built digital safety platform designed to replace the current paper-based SLAM (Stop, Look, Assess, Manage) re-entry procedure used by underground mining crews at Unki Mine following primary blasts.

The application digitizes Operational Procedure UNK-MIN-MIN-PRO-0002 (Version 6.0, last revised 2025-08-03) in its entirety — guiding Overseer Miners and their crews through all five procedural phases with real-time checklists, gas reading capture, hazard identification, a 30-minute countdown timer, and mandatory supervisor sign-off before any record is submitted.

Core purpose: Every re-entry record is timestamped, attributed to a named officer, and stored for a minimum retention period of 5 years — fully aligned with Valterra's Record Control policy and applicable legal requirements under the Mine (Management and Safety) Regulations S.I. 109 of 1990 and Explosives Regulations S.I. 72 of 1989.
02
The Problem with Paper

The current hard-copy SLAM procedure presents several operational and compliance risks that grow more significant as Valterra scales its underground operations.

Current State — Paper Forms
Forms are frequently completed retrospectively, not in real time
No enforcement mechanism to ensure all checklist steps are completed in sequence
Gas readings recorded manually with no validation or threshold alerts
Paper records are susceptible to loss, damage, and illegibility underground
No centralized audit trail accessible to supervisors or SHE management
30-minute time limit for initial re-entry cannot be actively monitored
Supervisor sign-off cannot be verified or timestamped independently
Future State — Digital App
Procedure completed live — each step requires active confirmation before progression
Sequential phase locking prevents steps from being skipped
Gas readings captured digitally with limit references displayed per field
All submissions stored securely in the cloud with full redundancy
SHE management dashboard provides real-time visibility of all records
Built-in 30-minute countdown timer with color-coded urgency alerts
Digital supervisor sign-off with automatic timestamp and identity attribution
03
How It Works — The Five Phases

The application mirrors the exact structure of Procedure UNK-MIN-MIN-PRO-0002. Each phase must be completed in full before the next phase is unlocked. Critical steps — those with direct safety or legal implications — are flagged visually and cannot be bypassed.

Phase Name Key Actions Critical Gates
9.1 Re-Entry Preparation Blast token check, watch synchronization, SCADA fan confirmation, 2-hour lapse verification, ventilation flap inspection 2-hr lapse confirmed · SCADA confirmed · Re-entry time on blasting board
9.2 Waiting Place Management Safety Talk, Toolbox Talk sign-in, Team Talk, Attendance Register, PPE inspection, fitness for duty, duty assignment Toolbox Talk signed · PPE inspected · All personnel fit for duty
9.3 Initial Re-Entry NSS Box locked to NO BLAST, barricade procedure, joint ground assessment, PCU disconnect, airflow verification, continuous gas testing, barricade installation NSS locked · PCU disconnected · Gas tests logged · 30-min timer enforced
9.4 Watering Down & Barring Down Gas test at blasted end, water blast operation (minimum 15 min), barring down per UNK-MIN-MIN-PRO-0003, ground condition logging No work under unsupported ground enforced
9.5 Searching & Handling Blown-Out Explosives Search for misfires and blown-out explosives, collection into Canvas/Chola bag, disposal into section explosives box, misfire reporting if applicable Blasting Licence holder only · Misfire escalation to PRO-0067
04
Key Features
⏱
30-Minute Countdown Timer

Live timer activates at Phase 3. Color transitions from green to amber to red as time elapses. Automatic alert if limit is exceeded.

🧪
Gas Reading Capture

Digital entry fields for CO, NO₂, O₂, and H₂S with legal threshold limits displayed. All readings stored per submission record.

⚠️
Hazard Identification

Structured hazard selection covering fumes, bad hangings, dust, blown-out explosives, ventilation issues, ground instability, and misfires.

📷
Photo Evidence Capture

Camera integration allows miners to attach photographic evidence to specific checklist steps — creating a visual audit record for SHE review.

✍️
Digital Supervisor Sign-Off

Mandatory sign-off step with supervisor name, digital confirmation, and automatic timestamp before any record is submitted and locked.

📊
SHE Management Dashboard

Web-based dashboard for Safety Officers and Section Managers to review, filter, search, and export all re-entry records by date, section, or officer.

📵
Offline Mode

Critical for underground environments where network connectivity is unreliable. Forms can be completed offline and automatically sync when signal is restored.

🔐
Role-Based Access

Overseer Miners, Shift Bosses, Safety Officers, and SHE Managers each see only what their role requires. All access is authenticated and logged.

Proposed Technology Stack

The application is built on modern, industry-proven technologies chosen for reliability, security, cost-effectiveness, and ease of maintenance.

Frontend
React Native (Expo)
Single codebase delivers native iOS and Android apps plus a web browser version — minimizing development cost and maintenance overhead.
Database
Supabase (PostgreSQL)
Open-source backend platform providing a structured relational database, real-time data sync, and file storage for photo evidence.
Auth
Supabase Auth
Role-based authentication ensuring only verified Valterra employees can access the system. Supports email, employee ID, or SSO integration.
Offline
Local Storage Sync
Forms saved locally on the device when underground without signal. Automatic background sync to the database when connectivity is restored.
Dashboard
React Web App
Browser-based SHE management dashboard. No installation required for supervisors and managers — accessible from any device on the mine network.

Value to Valterra

Beyond operational efficiency, the SLAM Re-Entry App delivers measurable value across three dimensions critical to Valterra's mining operations:

Safety: Guided sequential checklists eliminate the risk of steps being skipped under time pressure. Real-time gas readings with threshold references give crews immediate situational awareness. The 30-minute timer enforces the procedural time limit that is currently unenforceable on paper.
Compliance: Every submission creates an immutable, timestamped digital record. In the event of a safety incident, investigation, or regulatory audit, Valterra has a complete, searchable history of every re-entry conducted — who performed it, what was found, and who signed off.
Operational Intelligence: Aggregated data across all sections and shifts enables SHE management to identify patterns — recurring hazard types, sections with frequent gas exceedances, or teams with incomplete compliance. This insight is impossible with paper records.
